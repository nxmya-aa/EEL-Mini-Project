Designed for Scale

Features built to help municipalities and campus managers deploy efficient, data-driven collection systems.

Accurate Fill Sensing

Ultrasonic sensors measure content level without contact — works in low light and rainproof setups.

Wireless Telemetry

ESP modules push JSON packets to your server or third-party platforms (ThingSpeak, Firebase).

Alerts & Thresholds

Custom thresholds trigger SMS, email, or dashboard alerts for pickups.

Analytics & Routing

Use historical fullness profiles to build efficient pickup routes and shift frequency.

Energy Efficient

Low-power sleep modes and solar options for remote bins.

Open & Extensible

Modular hardware and open protocols allow future sensors (gas, weight) to be added.
